This page collects books, films, anime, and other things I still want to remember after reading, watching, or listening. Some are just short notes. Some may grow into full book reports.

## Full book reports

When a book changes how I think, or when its ideas are worth revisiting, I write a longer book report.

## Books

- **The Psychology of Money** — Morgan Housel  
  A good book for thinking about money, risk, long-term behavior, and ordinary investors.

- **Die with Zero** — Bill Perkins  
  A reminder that money is not just a number to maximize. It should be considered together with time, experience, family, and life stages.

## Films

- **Interstellar** — Christopher Nolan  
  A story about time, choice, and love. I often think about how it expresses the cost of time.

- **The Truman Show** — Peter Weir  
  A film about waking up, questioning a given script, and walking out of it.

- **Inception** — Christopher Nolan  
  I like how it makes a complex premise understandable, and how it talks about reality and obsession.

## Anime

- **Steins;Gate** — Nitroplus  
  Timelines, choices, and costs. It is a story worth revisiting.

- **Attack on Titan** — Hajime Isayama  
  Its power is not only in the worldbuilding, but in how it handles freedom, hatred, history, and perspective.

- **Violet Evergarden** — Kana Akatsuki  
  Beautifully made, and a gentle story about learning to understand emotions.
