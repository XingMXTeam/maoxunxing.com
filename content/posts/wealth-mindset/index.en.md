## Five Levels of Money Cognition

Your view of money determines your personal development level. If you want to solve money problems, you need to improve your cognitive level. The levels mentioned here can actually be divided into several points:
1. Survival
2. Status
3. Autonomy
4. Creativity
5. Contribution

### 1. Survival Stage

The survival stage is what we call how to solve life problems. Because of realistic pressures like mortgages or eating, you don't have the energy to think or do things you like. At this stage, you must do things for survival.

Most people are in the survival stage. And the survival stage is actually treating work, treating making money as work.

### 2. Status Stage

The second stage is called the status stage. The status stage means: you have reached a state where you have a sense of security about money, and then you need to make others feel you have value. At this stage, it's because you have a skill that can get you a better job, or you started a business that can pay life's bills. Then you can also enjoy some life, right? To feel this surface luxury.

### 3. Autonomy Stage

When you reach a certain degree from the status stage, you will find that this surface life bill payment and desires are not what you want. You may want to do some happier things, because after all, cars, mansions, watches will become outdated, and everything becomes not that important. At this time, you actually start to enter a "creativity stage".

### 4. Creativity Stage

At this stage, you have actually broken away from the process of learning skills, learning work methods or experiencing some training in the "status stage", and entered a kind of creation. At this stage you can try to feel different economic models, business models, or experience the value of real things; and you can start to have some ideas, to make some improvements.

When you combine the skills gained from the status stage with some ideas from your second creativity stage, you start to do something.

### 5. Contribution Stage

These things can be sharing your thoughts and ideas, sharing your cognition and views. When you go to be your most authentic self, actually you will have some contribution to society.

Because you are a real you, not someone who works for work, or does things others tell you to do for the survival stage. You are not an executor, you are an artist with ideas and creativity.

Others can always feel the most real you, and then you will feel that work is no longer work in the traditional sense, but a kind of rest and enjoyment, because you are really doing something you want to do.
