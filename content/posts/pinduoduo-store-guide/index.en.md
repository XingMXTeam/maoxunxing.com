I recently spent a lot of time researching how to open and run a store on Pinduoduo (PDD), one of China's largest e-commerce platforms known for its group-buying model and ultra-competitive pricing. From product selection to pricing, from shipping to after-sales service, I've made mistakes and learned valuable lessons along the way. This article doesn't deal in abstract theories — it records practical, actionable insights that can actually help you make money. Whether you're preparing to open your first store or already running one but still figuring things out, I hope these notes will be useful.

## Core Business Strategy: How Do You Make Money?

### The Profit Model

The core playbook on Pinduoduo can be summarized as: **broad categories + bulk listing + continuous sales + reasonable markup**.

Here's the simplest example: a product with a supply price of 10 RMB (including shipping) sells for 15 RMB, earning you 5 RMB net profit per order. With dozens of orders per day, making 2,000-3,000 RMB (~$300-400 USD) per month is quite achievable.

The advantages of this model are clear:

- No capital tie-up, no inventory risk
- Low investment, fast startup
- Dropshipping-friendly, asset-light operation

### Product Selection Strategy

#### Getting Started: Broad Categories + Test Listings

The most common mistake beginners make is over-focusing on a narrow niche. The right approach is:

1. **Choose broad, high-demand categories**: Daily necessities, home organization, student stationery, wedding supplies — stable demand with a wide audience.
2. **List 30-50 products initially**: All sourced from suppliers that support dropshipping (via 1688/Alibaba).
3. **Test with organic traffic**: Monitor product exposure and sales performance, gradually phasing out low-exposure, low-sales items.

**Important Warning**: Women's shoes and bags are major minefields. Avoid them as a beginner. Return rates are extremely high, quality disputes are common, and many suppliers refuse refunds.

#### Seasonal and Holiday-Driven Selection

Create a "holiday product calendar." Complete product listings, optimize main images, and set up keywords 7-14 days before a holiday or trending event. Also, delist time-sensitive products before shipping suspensions (e.g., before Chinese New Year).

#### AI-Assisted Product Selection

Use the platform's AI-powered "Business Opportunity" tab to find low-price blue-ocean products. But here's the catch: **when using AI search, you must individually verify each product's title, specifications, on-time shipping rate, repurchase rate, and positive review rate** to filter out products with high return rates, bad reviews, or delayed shipping.

## Listing Essentials: How to Avoid Losing Money

### Always Look for the Distribution + Free Shipping Label

As long as the product has **distribution + free shipping** capability, and you price based on the dropship price or smart price, you typically won't lose money on shipping.

**Anti-Loss Guide**: Downstream store -> Product selection -> Filter "dropshipping + free shipping."

### Choose Channel-Specific Best Sellers

For example, best sellers from the Pinduoduo-specific channel on 1688 are almost always free-shipping and profitable when listed on Pinduoduo.

## Pricing and Profit Optimization

### Pricing Strategy

1. **Start low to attract sales, then raise prices**: New buyers who join group purchases at the raised price generate your profit.
2. **Tiered price increases**: After selling 500 units, raise each SKU by 2 RMB. Once you've ordered enough from the same supplier, you'll have room to negotiate better wholesale prices.
3. **No paid promotion in the early stage**: Rely on organic traffic to test products first. Only consider paid promotion after finding stable, high-selling items with a per-unit gross profit of 15+ RMB.

### Profit Enhancement Tips

- **Turn losses into gains on large orders**: If your profit margin allows, include a few extra protective items or replacements to improve buyer experience and ratings.
- **Be cautious with shipping insurance**: While it improves customer experience, the ~0.5 RMB premium per order accumulates over time and eats into profits.

## Shipping and Logistics

### Shipping Timelines

Standardize your customer service responses:

- "Dear customer, this product is committed to ship within xx hours. Actual shipping times depend on logistics updates."
- "Dear customer, we select the courier based on your shipping address and daily logistics conditions. We cannot accept specific courier requests at this time."

### Out-of-Stock Handling

When inventory is running low, be proactive and transparent:

> "Dear customer, we're sorry to inform you that this product is currently running low on stock and may not ship in the short term. To avoid keeping you waiting, we recommend requesting a refund. We'll notify you as soon as it's restocked."

Or recommend alternatives:

> "Dear customer, the item you'd like is temporarily out of stock. We recommend requesting a refund first. Our store has several similar items with great value — feel free to browse. The refund process is quick and won't delay your reorder."

## After-Sales and Refunds

### Return Address Setup

- **Option A (Recommended)**: Use 1688's official return warehouse service — hassle-free but not free.
- **Option B**: Contact each supplier individually to obtain their return warehouse addresses and maintain them manually.

### Refund Processing

- **Refund after shipping**: After syncing after-sales information, the platform typically auto-triggers logistics interception with a high success rate.
- **No quality issues**: Return shipping is paid by the buyer.
- **Quality issues**: Return shipping should be covered by the 1688 supplier; include shipping costs in the refund claim.
- **After buyer returns**: Check the logistics tracking for the purchase order, and process the refund promptly after confirming the supplier has received the return.

### Automated After-Sales

The ISV backend allows you to configure automated after-sales handling. Refunds for unshipped or in-transit orders can be completed automatically. For return refunds, you can set the official warehouse as the return address or provide a supplier's address to the buyer.

## Customer Service Scripts

### Common Q&A

**Can I get a discount or free shipping?**

> "Dear customer, this is already the promotional price. You can follow our store for coupons to save even more on your next order."

Or make concessions based on your profit margin, but maintain consistent scripts to avoid disputes.

**Product quality or sizing issues?**

Always check the product detail page first:

> "Dear customer, the material/size details are listed on the product page. Please refer to those specifications. If you still have questions, feel free to send us a photo and we'll help confirm."

### A Practical Customer Service Trick

**Find the customer service for the same product on Pinduoduo, ask them the same question (they usually respond instantly), and then use their answer to respond to your own customer.** This is especially helpful when you're new and don't know the product details well.

## Risk Management

### Key Risk Warnings

1. **Buying high and selling low trap**: Upstream sellers might buy directly from your store at your lower price, confirm receipt immediately without waiting for delivery, and profit from the price difference — draining your funds.
2. **Beginners should be cautious with Pinduoduo promotions**: Taobao promotions are generally safe to join, but be careful with Pinduoduo events as they can lead to unexpected losses.
3. **Strict penalties are auto-enabled**: Fake-1-penalty-10 and 48-hour shipping penalty of at least 3 RMB are automatically applied to your store.

### Supplier Issue Handling

If a 1688 supplier causes delayed shipping or stock-out issues, the platform usually provides some compensation. However, 1688 currently has no additional compensation mechanism for compromised buyer experience on your end, which may result in net losses.

## Operational Tips

### Product Listing

- If you've already listed products, consider taking them all down and re-listing from the homepage's best-seller section or AI search results.
- Verify that store authorization is complete and that you selected "list immediately" during the listing process.
- If you selected "list to warehouse," you'll need to manually submit them in the backend.

### Bulk Purchasing

Use the 1688/Alibaba mobile app for batch payments — it's more efficient than processing orders one by one.

### After Products Start Selling

- Orders with positive profit margins can be auto-purchased by the system.
- Orders with negative profit margins require manual confirmation to prevent loss-making orders from processing automatically.
