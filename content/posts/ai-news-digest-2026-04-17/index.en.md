## 1. Core Model Releases & Technical Iterations

* **Claude Opus 4.7 Officially Launches with Performance Controversy**
    Anthropic has released the new generation Opus 4.7, which performs excellently on Vending-Bench 2. The official subscription limits have been raised to compensate for the consumption of its "long thinking." However, experts like Ethan Mollick point out that its "adaptive thinking" has flaws that cannot be manually intervened, often misjudging non-technical tasks as "low intelligence" requirements.
    (Source: [https://x.com/andonlabs/status/2044808572299878565](https://x.com/andonlabs/status/2044808572299878565))
    (Source: [https://x.com/emollick/status/2044864822076969268](https://x.com/emollick/status/2044864822076969268))
    (Source: [https://x.com/ClaudeDevs/status/2044868953206612154](https://x.com/ClaudeDevs/status/2044868953206612154))

* **Google Gemma 4 26B Now Available**
    Gemma 4 26B officially lands on Vertex AI MaaS, providing serverless access. Meanwhile, the SGLang team demonstrated inference acceleration solutions for this model at Gemma Party.
    (Source: [https://x.com/GoogleCloudTech/status/2044883532946825333](https://x.com/GoogleCloudTech/status/2044883532946825333))
    (Source: [https://x.com/lmsysorg/status/2044888006730072171](https://x.com/lmsysorg/status/2044888006730072171))

* **Ultra-Low Bit Model Bonsai Released**
    The Bonsai series models (8B/4B/1.7B) based on ternary weights (1.58 bits) have been released. The 8B version is only 2GB in size and can run at 60 tps in the browser on M4 Max, marking a major breakthrough in local edge AI.
    (Source: [https://x.com/xenovacom/status/2044834473874874519](https://x.com/xenovacom/status/2044834473874874519))
    (Source: [https://x.com/mervenoyann/status/2044841709075411047](https://x.com/mervenoyann/status/2044841709075411047))

## 2. AI Programming & Developer Tools

* **OpenAI Codex Evolves into "AI Operator"**
    OpenAI has updated Codex, transforming it from a programming assistant to a "workspace system." The new version supports parallel control of native Mac applications, image processing, and long-term automation tasks. Sam Altman called it "more useful than expected."
    (Source: [https://x.com/OpenAIDevs/status/2044828214867202519](https://x.com/OpenAIDevs/status/2044828214867202519))
    (Source: [https://x.com/sama/status/2044858862042591378](https://x.com/sama/status/2044858862042591378))

* **Cursor Research Report: Developers Are Transitioning to "Managers"**
    Cursor's research found that the proportion of high-difficulty tasks has increased by 68% over the past year, with developers spending significantly more time on architecture design, code review, and documentation.
    (Source: [https://x.com/cursor_ai/status/2044841478913130930](https://x.com/cursor_ai/status/2044841478913130930))

* **LangChain Launches Reusable Evaluation Templates**
    LangSmith now supports over 30 preset evaluation templates, covering "model as judge" and rule-based code evaluation.
    (Source: [https://x.com/LangChain/status/2044824090205360393](https://x.com/LangChain/status/2044824090205360393))
