## 🎯 Core Business Strategy

### Profit Model
- **Wide Category + Continuous Sales + Reasonable Markup**
- Example: Purchase price 10 yuan (free shipping), sell for 15 yuan, net profit 5 yuan per order
- With dozens of daily sales, monthly income of 2,000-3,000 yuan is achievable
- Advantages: No inventory pressure, no inventory risk, low investment, quick startup

### Product Selection Strategy
- **Initial Stage**: Use "Wide Category + Broad Testing" strategy
  - Avoid over-focusing on narrow sub-categories
  - Prioritize broader, more stable categories (daily necessities, home storage, student stationery, wedding supplies, etc.)
  - Initially list 30-50 products supporting dropshipping
  - Observe product exposure and sales performance through natural traffic, gradually eliminate low-exposure and low-sales products

- **Seasonal and Holiday Driven**:
  - Establish a "Holiday Selection Calendar"
  - Complete product listing, main image optimization, and keyword layout 7-14 days before holidays or hot events
  - Delist time-sensitive products before logistics shutdown

- **AI-Assisted Product Selection**:
  - Use the "absolute low-price blue ocean products" in the AI version's "Business Opportunities" tab
  - Product selection includes multi-variable decisions such as pricing, trend prediction, and supply fulfillment

## 📦 Platform Features and Considerations

### Pinduoduo Platform Characteristics
- **Huge price-sensitive traffic pool**: New stores with price advantages can scale up very quickly with high conversion efficiency
- **No support for batch flexible pricing and synchronized shipping templates**: High learning curve
- **Strict price comparison mechanism**: Requires materials and manual pricing to achieve high profit margins
- **Strict risk control**: Fake 1 penalty 10, at least 3 yuan penalty for not shipping within 48 hours

### Product Listing Essentials
- **Must recognize the dropshipping free shipping label**: As long as the product has dropshipping free shipping capability and pricing references the dropshipping price or smart price, you usually won't incur losses due to shipping costs
- **Loss Prevention Guide**: Downstream store - Product selection - Filter dropshipping + free shipping
- **Select corresponding channel's hot products**: For example, products from Pinduoduo's special hot section are basically free shipping and profitable when listed on Pinduoduo

## 💰 Pricing and Profit Optimization

### Pricing Strategy
- First attract orders with low prices then raise prices, new buyers will order at the raised price and make a profit
- Raise price by 2 yuan per SKU after selling 500 units, you have bargaining power when purchasing certain quantities from the same supplier
- Can use volume model or guaranteed ROI model

### Profit Enhancement Techniques
- Seize opportunities to turn large orders profitable: In cases where profit is acceptable, send extra damaged-gift protection items to improve buyer experience
- Carefully activate shipping insurance: Although it improves experience, the premium of about 0.5 yuan per order will erode profits over time

## 🚚 Shipping and Logistics

### Shipping Time
- Reply template: "Dear, this product promises to ship within xx hours, actual logistics update shall prevail."
- Unified reply for courier questions: "Dear, we will choose the courier based on your order address and the day's actual logistics situation, we don't accept specified couriers for now."

### Out of Stock Handling
- Out of stock reply templates:
  - "Dear, very sorry to inform you that this product is currently out of stock and may not be able to ship in the short term 😔 To avoid making you wait, we suggest you apply for a refund first. We will notify you as soon as it's restocked. Really sorry for the trouble!"
  - "Dear, the one you want to change is temporarily out of stock 😔 We suggest you refund first, our store has several similar styles with high cost performance, you can see if there's anything you like～Refund process is quick, won't delay your re-ordering."

## 🔄 After-sales and Refunds

### Return Address Settings
- **Option A (Recommended)**: Use 1688 official return warehouse service, worry-free and effort-saving
- **Option B**: Actively contact each supplier to request return warehouse address, maintain individually

### Refund Processing
- **Refund after shipping**: After synchronizing after-sales information, the platform usually automatically triggers logistics interception, with high actual success rate
- **No quality issues**: Return shipping costs are borne by the user
- **Quality issues**: Return shipping costs should be borne by the 1688 supplier, shipping costs can be included in the refund
- **After buyer returns**: Check the logistics trajectory of that purchase order in the order details page, confirm the merchant has signed for it and process the refund in time

### Automatic After-sales Settings
- ISV backend can set automatic after-sales
- Unshipped refunds or in-transit refunds can be completed automatically
- Return refunds can set official warehouse as return address, or ask merchant for an address to send to buyer

## 📞 Customer Service Scripts

### Common Question Replies
- **Can you offer discount/free shipping**:
  - "Dear, this is already the promotional price, you can follow the store coupons to get more discounts when ordering～"
  - Or make appropriate concessions based on profit space, but suggest unified scripts to avoid disputes

- **Product quality issues/size issues**:
  - First confirm if the product details page has relevant instructions
  - "Dear, the material/size of this product is explained in detail in the product details page, please refer to it. If you still have questions, you can send pictures to help me confirm～"

### Customer Service Tips
- You can find Pinduoduo same-product customer service to consult the same question, usually instant reply, then use it to reply to your customers

## ⚠️ Risk Prevention

### Important Risk Warnings
- **High purchase price, low selling price**: Upstream bosses may buy directly from you, receive goods immediately after placing orders, don't even need to ship, fleece you
- **Beginners should carefully participate in Pinduoduo activities**: Taobao and other activities can be participated in
- **Fake 1 penalty 10, at least 3 yuan penalty for not shipping within 48 hours**: Store automatically enabled these rules

### Supplier Issue Handling
- If 1688 merchants have delayed shipping, out of stock, etc., the platform usually provides corresponding compensation
- 1688 currently has no additional compensation mechanism for user experience damage, this part may cause losses

## 🛠️ Operation Techniques

### Product Listing
- If already listed, suggest delisting all, then select products from homepage hot section or AI search (Pinduoduo xxx one piece free shipping) to relist
- Check if store authorization is completed, and whether "immediate listing" was selected when listing products
- If "list to warehouse" was selected, need to manually submit in backend

### Batch Purchasing
- Go to Alibaba APP to batch pay

### After Product Orders
- Orders with positive profit can be automatically purchased by the system
- Orders with negative profit need manual confirmation (to avoid loss orders automatically running through)
