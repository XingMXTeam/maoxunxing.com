I am Felix Mao, also known online as maoxunxing and 毛毛星.

I am a senior front-end engineer with more than 10 years of experience in big tech companies including NetEase and Alibaba. I write about AI tools, AI coding, indie hacking, creator workflows, personal knowledge systems, and long-term investing.

This site is not just a personal diary. It is my public knowledge node: a place where I document how AI, code, media, and investing can become a higher-leverage personal system.

## What I write about

### AI indie hacking

I study how AI lowers the cost of building products, writing content, automating workflows, and distributing ideas as a solo creator or small team.

### AI coding and frontend engineering

As a long-time web frontend engineer, I test tools such as Cursor, Claude Code, Codex, Gemini CLI, OpenCode, Vercel, and Hugo in real development workflows.

### Creator workflows

I publish and experiment across WeChat, Xiaohongshu, YouTube, Substack, and this website. I care about turning raw ideas into articles, videos, covers, publishing packages, and durable assets.

### Long-term investing and systems thinking

I write about index funds, technology ETFs, Bitcoin, cash flow, long-term compounding, and practical asset allocation for ordinary people.

## Who this site is for

This site is for developers, creators, indie hackers, and anyone who wants to use AI to amplify personal capability.

If you are looking for a first-person record of how a technical creator builds leverage with AI, code, media, and investing, this site is the archive I am building in public.
